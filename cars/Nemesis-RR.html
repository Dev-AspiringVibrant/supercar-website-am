Trion Supercars

Nemesis RR

The RR

The Trion Nemesis RR is an extraordinary hypercar that was first unveiled at the Silicon Valley Auto Show in January 2016. This vehicle boasts an impressive 1,500 kW power output and is capable of reaching a staggering top speed of over 270 mph (approximately 435 km/h). With a remarkable acceleration from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.8 seconds, the Nemesis RR is designed to compete at the highest levels of automotive performance. The latest iteration, the 2025 Trion Nemesis RR, combines breathtaking power with unmatched elegance, showcasing advanced engineering and an all-electric powertrain that emphasizes high performance while maintaining zero emissions. This hypercar not only represents a significant achievement in automotive technology but also aims to redefine the standards of luxury and speed in the hypercar segment.
