Trion Supercars

Black Widow

Trion is redefining its future with the Black Widow supercar, a groundbreaking leap in automotive innovation. This high-performance vehicle showcases Trion's commitment to cutting-edge technology, luxury, and sustainability, delivering over 1,400 horsepower with advanced axial flux electric motors. The Black Widow combines a sleek aerodynamic design with an all-electric powertrain, reflecting Trion's vision of where high performance meets environmental responsibility. More than just a supercar, it represents the company's bold ambition to lead the next generation of electric vehicles, blending power, precision, and eco-conscious engineering to set a new standard in luxury performance.

Specifications

  • Engine: 7.2L V8
  • Horsepower: 1.400
  • Top Speed: 500 km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: 1.8 seconds
  • Price: $2.5 million
  • Production: 30 units
